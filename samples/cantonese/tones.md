# Tone Demonstration

Cantonese has 6 lexical tones. The Tengwar mode marks them with contour shapes and register modifiers.

## The Classic и©© (si) Set - All 6 Tones

| Jyutping | зІөеӯ— | English | Tone Description | Tengwar | Romanized Names |
|----------|------|---------|------------------|---------|-----------------|
| si1 | и©© | poem | high level (55) | оҖғоҒ„оҒҗ | `{thule}[i]_` |
| si2 | еҸІ | history | high rising (35) | оҖғоҒ„оҒҶ | `{thule}[i][e/rise]` |
| si3 | и©Ұ | try | mid level (33) | оҖғоҒ„оҒҗоҒ… | `{thule}[i]_[a/.]` |
| si4 | жҷӮ | time | low falling (21) | оҖғоҒ„оҒ”оҒғ | `{thule}[i]\..` |
| si5 | еёӮ | market | low rising (23) | оҖғоҒ„оҒҶоҒғ | `{thule}[i][e/rise]..` |
| si6 | дәӢ | matter | low level (22) | оҖғоҒ„оҒҗоҒғ | `{thule}[i]_..` |

## Tone Mark Explanation

| Tone | Contour | Register | Mark Description |
|------|---------|----------|------------------|
| 1 (high level) | flat bar | high | flat bar only |
| 2 (high rising) | rising | high | acute stroke only |
| 3 (mid level) | flat bar | mid | flat bar + single dot |
| 4 (low falling) | falling | low | grave stroke + double dot |
| 5 (low rising) | rising | low | acute stroke + double dot |
| 6 (low level) | flat bar | low | flat bar + double dot |

## Checked (Entering) Tones
