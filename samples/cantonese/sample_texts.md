# Cantonese Sample Texts

Reference texts for Tengwar-Cantonese rendering. Romanization uses Jyutping with tone numbers (1-6).

---

## 5. Tone Demonstration Phrases

Cantonese has 6 lexical tones (some analyses count 9 including checked tones).

### The Classic 詩 (si) Set - All 6 Tones

| Jyutping | 粤字 | English | Tone Description |
|----------|------|---------|------------------|
| si1 | 詩 | poem | high level (55) |
| si2 | 史 | history | high rising (35) |
| si3 | 試 | try | mid level (33) |
| si4 | 時 | time | low falling (21) |
| si5 | 市 | market | low rising (23) |
| si6 | 事 | matter | low level (22) |

### The 分 (fan) Set

| Jyutping | 粤字 | English | Tone |
|----------|------|---------|------|
| fan1 | 分 | divide | 1 |
| fan2 | 粉 | powder | 2 |
| fan3 | 訓 | teach | 3 |
| fan4 | 焚 | burn | 4 |
| fan5 | 憤 | angry | 5 |
| fan6 | 份 | portion | 6 |

### Checked (Entering) Tones

Short syllables ending in -p, -t, -k inherit tone contours from tones 1, 3, 6.

| Jyutping | 粤字 | English | Tone Type |
|----------|------|---------|-----------|
| sik1 | 色 | color | high checked (from T1) |
| sik3 | 識 | know | mid checked (from T3) |
| sik6 | 食 | eat | low checked (from T6) |

### Minimal Tone Pairs in Context

| Jyutping | 粤字 | English |
|----------|------|---------|
| maai5 je5 | 買嘢 | buy things |
| maai6 je5 | 賣嘢 | sell things |
| gam1 | 金 | gold |
| gam2 | 咁 | so (adverb) |
| gam3 | 禁 | prohibit |
