## Notes

The Chinese translation renders the parallelism of the original elegantly:
- 至尊戒 (zhì zūn jiè) = "supreme ring" / "sovereign ring"
- 驭 (yù) = "to control, to drive" → rule
- 寻 (xún) = "to seek, to find" → find
- 引 (yǐn) = "to lead, to draw" → bring
- 禁锢 (jìn gù) = "to imprison, to shackle" → bind
- 黑暗中 (hēi àn zhōng) = "in the darkness"
